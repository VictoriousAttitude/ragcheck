# Incident response

Incidents are coordinated in a dedicated channel by the on-call responder.

## Severity levels

Severity one is a full outage or data-loss risk and requires an incident
commander within fifteen minutes. Severity two covers degraded service for
many tenants. Severity three is a contained problem with a known workaround
and is handled during business hours.

## Escalation

The escalation path goes from the service on-call to the team lead and then
to the engineering director. Paging is automatic when an alert stays
unacknowledged for ten minutes.

## Postmortems

A postmortem is required for every severity-one and severity-two incident.
The document is due within five business days and follows a blameless format
focused on contributing causes and action items with owners.
