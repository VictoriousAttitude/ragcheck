# Billing

Usage is metered hourly and invoiced monthly.

## Invoices

Invoices are generated on the first day of each month and cover the previous
month's usage. The billing service retries failed charges three times over
seven days before opening a support ticket and suspending the account.

## Plans

The starter plan is free and limited to one project with two seats. The team
plan adds unlimited projects, audit logs, and priority support. Enterprise
agreements are negotiated individually and include a dedicated support
engineer.

## Refunds

Refund requests are accepted within thirty days of the invoice date. Approved
refunds are returned to the original payment method within five business days.
